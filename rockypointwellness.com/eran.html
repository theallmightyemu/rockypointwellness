Dr. Eran Even, Dr. TCM, R.Ac, Owner book an appointment

eran@rockypointwellness.com

Currently a local resident and business owner in the Tri-Cities, Eran Even has lived, studied and worked in communities around Canada, Europe, Israel and the Peoples Republic of China. He brings a breadth of experience and expertise to the community with Rocky Point Wellness Clinic.

In 2003, Dr. Even graduated from the five-year Chinese medical program at the Shang Hai Traditional Chinese Medicine College in Vancouver and spent the following year interning with local Traditional Chinese Medical (TCM) doctors. That same year, he began formal studies of the Chinese language in order to access the extensive breadth of information only available in Chinese.

In 2005 Dr. Even travelled to the Peoples Republic of China to complete graduate training, (in Chinese), at the Beijing University of Chinese Medicine. Dr. Even mentored under one of China's great acupuncturists, Dr. Yu Lan Bai, an internal medicine specialist and an expert in abdominal acupuncture.

In 2013 Eran returned to China to train with Professor Huang, a world renowned specialist in Classical Chinese medicine at the Nanjing University of Chinese Medicine. He returned again in 2015 to begin his Ph.D studies with Professor Huang as his clinical advisor.

In January 2015, Eran completed a two year program in Classical Chinese medicine through the Institute of Classics in East Asian Medicine in Portland, Oregon, earning the designation of Diplomate in Canonical Chinese Medicine.

Eran's translations have been published in several journals and publications including, The Lantern, The Register of Chinese Herbal Medicine, The International Journal of Integrative Oncology, and The Health Action Networks Health Action magazine. Eran's translation of a seminal Chinese medical text was published in September, 2014 through the Chinese Medicine Database, and he is currently working on the translation of another classical Chinese medical textbook.

Dr. Even is a registered Acupuncturist and Doctor of Traditional Chinese Medicine in good standing with the College of Traditional Chinese Medicine Practitioners and Acupuncturists of British Columbia (CTCMA). He also holds professional certification in Western Herbal Medicine.
Eran lives in Coquitlam with his wife and two daughters.
