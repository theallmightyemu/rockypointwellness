Kona

Animals have been accepted into our homes as family members for quite some time. Animals can help reduce symptoms of stress, anxiety and depression simply by being a part of our life. Dogs for an example can make someone smile simply by wagging their tail; no matter how tough are day is they are always happy to see us. They also motivate us to get moving and get outside. Simply spending time in the moment with our pet’s unconditional acceptance and love can have a positive effect on our mood.

In therapy, just like in our home, the presence of an animal can assist in the therapeutic process by helping to create a calm, safe place to work through challenges that are impacting enjoyment in life. We are lucky to have our very own therapy dog here at Rocky Point Wellness who is always ready to greet our clients with a wagging tail.

Kona is a 5-year-old Golden Retriever who loves life and is happy to be outside exploring or inside relaxing with people. Kona is calm natured, affectionate and loves people of all ages. She is very social and loves to say hello to anyone coming her way.

Kona is known for her gentle and loving approach when helping children and adults feel more comfortable working towards individual therapy goals. Kona is very patient, loving and enjoys being available to those who require her help to move beyond barriers that are preventing positive change and growth in life.
