Yoga with Kasia is an intimate, home based yoga studio in the Glenayre neighborhood of Port Moody. Kasia offers a variety of classes for both beginner and advanced students. She specializes in prenatal and postnatal yoga and feels privileged to work with expecting and new mothers. Kasia is also passionate about Restorative Yoga which she believes is essential for people with busy lives. A truly relaxing and healing practice to reconnect and to access the self healing energy within. Yoga with Kasia gives you the rare experience of a very personal practice in a safe and warm environment. Kasia is also pleased to now offer one-on-one Restorative yoga classes at Rocky Point Wellness.

We infuse oils with Chinese herbs, which have been used for centuries, to repair damage caused by the sun and pollution, maintain moisture and enhance the skin. All of our products have been tested on willing two-legged humans, none on animals - we don't believe in doing that.
Our products are all free from preservatives and chemicals.
Our emphasis is on transparency, safe ingredients and purity.
