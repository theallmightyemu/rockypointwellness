Dr. Ryan Takagi, DC book an appointment

ryan@rockypointwellness.com

Raised in a small town, Dr. Ryan Takagi is a Chiropractor with a love for learning. He earned his Bachelors of Kinesiology from the University of British Columbia then went on to complete his Doctor of Chiropractic (summa cum laude) at the University of Western States in Portland, OR. He is currently completing a Masters of Science in Human Nutrition and Functional Medicine.

With the help of Chiropractic manipulation, various soft tissue therapies, rehabilitative exercises, nutritional counseling, and education, Ryan will work with you to optimize your biomechanics and physiology so you may reach your full potential in any environment. Whether it's at home, in the workplace, or on the sports field. Dr. Takagi wants to help you lay down the foundations for healthy and happy habits that radiate over the lifespan and through the community. As a Chiropractor, his specialty is physical health and well-being. His additional training in nutrition and functional medicine allows him to explore physical health within the complex and interacting web of other biological and environmental systems.

Ryan's approach includes an in-depth assessment followed by personalized management options or an appropriate referral.

In his spare time, Dr. Takagi enjoys shredding an old pair of runners on a new set of trails and coaching; currently he has the pleasure of coaching the SFU Rowing Novice Crew.
